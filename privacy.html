Privacy policy

What information do we store?

Attendees of Homebrew Website Club London who register through Tito must supply their name and email address during the registration process. We will only use this information for the purpose to confirm how many people intend on attending the event and to be able to identify those attending at the venue. Unless an attendee has opted-in to be kept informed of future HWC events then these details will not be stored beyond the the date that the event they've registered for has occured.

No other information is collected from users of this website and any future changes that could affect this policy will be added to this page.

For further information or requests about the data we hold please email the event organiser hwc@calumryan.com
